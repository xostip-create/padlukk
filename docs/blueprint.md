# **App Name**: Padluckk Editorial Platform

## Core Features:

- Curated Content Display: Showcase editorial pieces including articles, photo essays, and videos in a visually compelling and easily navigable layout.
- Category Filtering and Search: Enable users to filter content by category (e.g., fashion, art, music) and search for specific topics or creators.
- Creator Spotlights: Feature individual creators and their work, providing a platform for discovery and recognition.
- Contact Form: Contact form with captcha protection that emails content requests and messages to the team.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Refined gold (#BFA14D), suggesting quality.
- Background color: Off-white (#F2F0EC), creating a subtle, sophisticated backdrop.
- Accent color: Muted Olive (#7A8347), adding an organic touch, but remaining refined.
- Body font: 'Literata', a transitional serif, bestowing a literary feel.
- Headline font: 'Belleza', a humanist sans-serif well suited to content about art and design.
- Use minimalist line icons to represent categories and navigation elements.
- Emphasize white space and clean lines for a modern, uncluttered aesthetic.
- Subtle transitions and animations to enhance user experience without being distracting.
